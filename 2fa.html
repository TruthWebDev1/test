Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your TruthWeb account with Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). Add an extra layer of security by requiring a second verification step beyond your password, ensuring only you can access your Pi wallet and data.

How It Works

1. Enter Password

Log in with your usual credentials.

2. Verify Device

Receive a code via SMS or app.

3. Secure Access

Enter the code to gain access.

Why Enable 2FA?

Enhanced Protection

Safeguards against password theft.

Peace of Mind

Know your account is secure.

Quick Setup

Enable it in just a few steps.

How to Enable 2FA

  1. Go to your Profile.
  2. Navigate to the "Security" section.
  3. Select "Enable 2FA" and choose your method (SMS or Authenticator App).
  4. Follow the prompts to link your phone number or scan the QR code.
  5. Save your backup codes in a secure place.
  6. Confirm setup with the verification code sent to your device.
